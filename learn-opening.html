Basic Principles in the Opening

In starting a chess game you must know the basic opening principles. These principles are also applied in high-level chess. It is important that you don't skip this part of your practice

Control the Center

In early stage of the game the two players will fight for the control in center. If you are dominating the center then you can start your attack because your pieces are well placed

Develop Minor Pieces

Attacking in chess requires the development of your pieces. Before attacking you must bring more attackers in the game and these are the minor pieces.

Castle early

Putting your king to safety in every game gives you higher chance in winnning the game. Both of the players wants to attack the enemy king so your solution is keep your king on the side of the board

Connect the rooks

The rooks are better if they are defending each other. Here it is safe to say that you have finished your development if your rooks are connected.
